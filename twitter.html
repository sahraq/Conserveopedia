Twitter Data Practices

How Twitter Harvests Your Data

Twitter collects data about your tweets, location, and who you interact with. It uses this information to display targeted ads and recommendations.

Major Data Breaches

In 2020, Twitter experienced a breach where hackers accessed high-profile accounts and compromised private information.

Privacy Violations

Twitter has faced accusations for tracking users' web activity across different sites through embedded tweets.

Unsettling Facts

  • Twitter collects information on users’ interactions with tweets, including likes, replies, and retweets.
  • Twitter faced backlash for collecting and storing users' phone numbers for targeted ads, even when users opted out.
