How Twitter Harvests Your Data

Twitter collects information such as your tweets, messages, followers, and even location data. This data is used for ad targeting and improving the platform’s algorithms.

Notable Data Breaches

  • 2013 Data Breach: Hackers gained access to Twitter’s internal systems, affecting some high-profile accounts.
  • 2020 Twitter Hack: Hackers gained control of several high-profile accounts, highlighting vulnerabilities in Twitter’s security.

Privacy Concerns

Twitter’s data collection is heavily used for personalized advertising, but there are concerns about how much data is being collected without users’ knowledge or consent.
