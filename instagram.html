Instagram Data Practices

How Instagram Harvests Your Data

Instagram collects data from user posts, interactions, location, and more. This data is used for personalized ads and recommendations on the platform.

Major Data Breaches

In 2019, Instagram suffered a breach that exposed phone numbers and email addresses of 49 million users.

Privacy Violations

Instagram was criticized for allowing third-party apps to access user data without permission and not providing enough control over what data users shared.

Unsettling Facts

  • Instagram tracks users' interactions on its platform and even with non-users to collect data on consumer behavior.
  • Instagram uses facial recognition technology for automatically tagging photos, which has raised privacy concerns.
