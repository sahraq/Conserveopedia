LinkedIn Data Practices

How LinkedIn Harvests Your Data

LinkedIn collects professional data about users’ work history, connections, and job searches. This data is used for advertising and recruitment purposes.

Major Data Breaches

LinkedIn faced a data breach in 2021, where 700 million users' personal data was compromised.

Privacy Violations

LinkedIn tracks users' activity across the web through its "LinkedIn Insight Tag" to serve ads to potential job seekers or recruiters.

Unsettling Facts

  • LinkedIn has been accused of storing personal data indefinitely, even if users delete their profiles.
  • The platform's data-sharing with Microsoft raised concerns about privacy and user data control.
