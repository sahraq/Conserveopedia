TikTok Data Practices

How TikTok Harvests Your Data

TikTok collects data including your browsing history, location, and user interactions to serve tailored advertisements.

Major Data Breaches

In 2020, a TikTok bug exposed private videos, with over 3 billion users' data potentially at risk.

Privacy Violations

TikTok has been scrutinized for its data collection practices, especially regarding minors' personal information.

Unsettling Facts

  • TikTok tracks user activity both on and off the app, raising concerns about surveillance.
  • There are accusations that TikTok collects biometric data without users' explicit consent.
